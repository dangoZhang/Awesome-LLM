# Retrieval-Augmented Generation

RAG moved from simple retrieve-then-generate pipelines toward modular,
graph-based, and agentic systems.

## Surveys and Position Papers

- (2020-05) [Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.11401) - original RAG paper.
- (2023-12) [Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Large Language Models: A Survey](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.10997) - naive, advanced, and modular RAG taxonomy.
- (2024-07) [Modular RAG: Transforming RAG Systems into LEGO-like Reconfigurable Frameworks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.21059) - reusable modules and operators.
- (2025-01) [Agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation: A Survey on Agentic RAG](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.09136) - autonomous and multi-agent RAG systems.
- (2025-06) [A Survey on Reasoning Agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.10408) - reasoning workflows, tool use, and dynamic retrieval.
